# User Guide - Accounts and Contacts

## Purpose

Accounts represent organisations. Contacts represent people. Contacts can be linked to accounts and used on leads, opportunities, cases, activities, and documents.

## Creating an Account

1. Open Relationships > Accounts.
2. Select New.
3. Enter Account Name.
4. Add phone, website, relationship type, and parent account if relevant.
5. Add address and profile details.
6. Save.

## Creating a Contact

1. Open Relationships > Contacts.
2. Select New.
3. Enter first name, last name, job title, email, phone, and mobile.
4. Link the contact to an account.
5. Add LinkedIn profile URL if known.
6. Save.

## Field Notes

- `dove365_linkedinprofile`: stores the LinkedIn profile URL and can render the LinkedIn Badge PCF.
- `dove365_convertedfromlead`: shows the lead used to create or link the contact after conversion.
- `dove365_marketingpreferences`: records marketing content preferences where used.
