# Training Material

## Admin Training

Objectives:

- Understand solution structure and import order.
- Configure environment variables and connection references.
- Assign CRM roles and share the app.
- Maintain service offerings.
- Monitor flows, mailboxes, and SharePoint documents.

Suggested agenda:

1. Architecture and dependency overview.
2. Configuration walkthrough.
3. Role assignment and app sharing.
4. Service offering maintenance.
5. Support and troubleshooting.

Hands-on exercise:

- Configure a service offering and confirm a standard user can select it on an opportunity.

- Convert qualified leads.
- Track opportunities through the sales process.
- Add service offerings for reporting.

## Case and Support Training

Objectives:

- Create and triage cases.
- Track priority, type, target date, and status.
- Escalate cases.
- Record resolution.

Practice scenario:

- A customer raises a support request linked to an account, contact, opportunity, and service offering. The case is escalated, resolved, and closed.

Expected outcomes:

- Users understand which record type to use.
- Users can follow the BPF stages.
- Users can find related activities and documents.
- Admins know how to diagnose common configuration issues.
