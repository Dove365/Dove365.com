# User Guide - Leads

## Purpose

Leads capture early-stage prospects before they are ready to become opportunities or contacts.

Known process flow: 1. New → 2. Research → 3. Engage → 4. Qualify → 5. Convert (or 5. Close).

Known statuses: Active, Converted, Inactive.

## Create a Lead

1. Open Pipeline > Leads.
2. Select New.
3. Add lead name, person details, account prospect name, email, phone, source, and address where available.
4. Save.

## Engage with a Lead

Record calls, emails, tasks, and notes on the timeline. Set temperature to Hot, Warm, Cold, or Dead to help prioritise follow-up.

Use the Qualify stage when there is enough evidence that the lead should progress. Set the qualify flag and add relevant notes.

## Interpreting Communication History

Activities and emails on the timeline show the relationship history. Review the timeline before deciding whether to convert, close, or continue nurturing the lead.
