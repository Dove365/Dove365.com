How Dove365 uses cookies, similar technologies, and Cookiebot consent controls on this website.

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This page explains how cookies are used on this website, including Google Analytics where consented to, and provides the live Cookiebot cookie declaration for the current domain.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help a site work correctly, remember choices, understand usage, or support embedded third-party services.

How We Use Cookies

Dove365 uses Cookiebot to manage cookie consent. Depending on the services active on the website, cookies may be used for:

  • Necessary purposes: to support basic website operation, security, consent storage, and page functionality.
  • Preference purposes: to remember choices that improve your browsing experience.
  • Statistics purposes: to understand how visitors use the site, where analytics are enabled and consented to.
  • Marketing purposes: to support marketing or embedded third-party content, where enabled and consented to.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help understand how visitors use this website and to improve our content, services, and user experience. Google Analytics is provided by Google Ireland Limited. It may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information such as pages visited, referral source, approximate location derived from IP address, device and browser information, language settings, interaction events, and time spent on pages.

Google Analytics 4 may set first-party cookies such as _ga, used to distinguish users, and _ga_<container-id>, used to persist session state. The exact cookies and durations shown in the Cookiebot declaration may change if our analytics setup changes.

Google Analytics is treated as a statistics/analytics service and should only run where you have given the relevant consent through Cookiebot. We do not intentionally send names, email addresses, form message contents, or other directly identifying information to Google Analytics.

Google may process analytics information on servers outside the UK or EEA. More information about how Google handles business data is available at Google Business Data Responsibility.

Forms, Spam Protection and Automation

Website forms are handled using Netlify Forms and may be processed through Microsoft Power Automate for internal notifications, routing, follow-up tasks, and delivery of requested resources or reports. These tools may process form submission metadata needed to receive, secure, and route your submission.

Some forms use anti-spam measures such as hidden honeypot fields and Netlify reCAPTCHA. Where reCAPTCHA or similar Google services are active, Google may use cookies or similar technologies for fraud prevention and risk management. Any cookies detected on the current domain will be shown in the Cookiebot declaration below where applicable.
