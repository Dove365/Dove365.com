# User Guide - Activities and Timelines

## Purpose

Activities and timelines show communication and work history across CRM records. Use them to understand what happened, what is planned, and who is responsible.

## When to Use Activities

Use activities for:

- Phone calls.
- Emails.
- Tasks.
- Appointments.
- Notes.

## Working with Timeline

1. Open a record such as Account, Contact, Lead, Opportunity, or Case.
2. Find the Timeline area.
3. Add notes or activities.
4. Review historical communication before making decisions.

## Best Practice

- Record meaningful subject lines.
- Complete activities when done.
- Use notes for important context that does not belong in a field.
- Check timeline before converting a lead, closing an opportunity, or resolving a case.
