# User Guide - Overview

## Purpose

Dove365 CRM Starter helps teams manage relationships, leads, opportunities, service offerings, cases, activities, and documents in a simple model-driven app.

## Navigation

The app is organised into:

- Dashboard: CRM summary and activity dashboard.
- Relationship: Accounts and Contacts.
- Pipeline: Leads and Opportunities.
- Service: Cases and Service Offerings.

## Core Records

- Account: an organisation or company.
- Contact: a person.
- Lead: an unqualified prospect or enquiry.
- Opportunity: a qualified sales opportunity.
- Service Offering: a service that can be attached to opportunities and cases.
- Case: a support request, issue, enquiry, or service matter.

## Best Practice

- Keep accounts and contacts clean before creating opportunities or cases.
- Use leads for early-stage prospects.
- Convert leads only when there is enough information to create or link a contact/account.
- Add activities to keep communication history clear.
- Use service offerings consistently for pipeline and support reporting.
