What It Is

Everything your team needs in one system

Dove365 CRM — Starter is a productised CRM solution that runs inside Microsoft Power Platform. It manages accounts, contacts, leads, opportunities, cases, and activities — structured around how your business works, not a generic template.

01

What it is

A fully configured, ready-to-deploy CRM built on Microsoft Dataverse — Microsoft's enterprise data platform. Not a SaaS subscription. Not a disconnected spreadsheet. A structured system inside your Microsoft 365 environment.

02

Who it's for

Designed for small and medium businesses that want a structured, scalable CRM without the complexity of Dynamics 365 or Salesforce. Built for teams already using Microsoft 365 who need to bring order to their customer relationships.

03

Key benefits

A single structured system for all customer data. Built-in Outlook and SharePoint integration. Enterprise-grade role-based security. Unlimited users. One-off price. Deployed and configured by Dove365.

04

Common use cases

Managing a sales pipeline. Tracking customer support cases. Coordinating client onboarding. Replacing disconnected spreadsheets and inboxes with a single source of truth your whole team can rely on.

Features

Built to cover your full customer lifecycle

Dove365 CRM — Starter includes everything your team needs to manage customer relationships from first contact through to ongoing support — out of the box.

✓

Accounts & Contacts

Your core customer database — every organisation and person you work with, linked together in a structured, searchable record with complete history.

Lead Management

Capture, research, qualify, and convert prospects with a guided 5-stage business process flow. Never lose track of where a lead stands.

Opportunity Management

Track your sales pipeline through qualification, proposal, negotiation, and close. Dashboards show pipeline value, stage, and forecast at a glance.

Case Management

Handle support requests and service enquiries from creation to resolution. Categorise work using a shared service catalogue for consistent tracking.

✓

Activities & Timeline

Log emails, calls, tasks, and appointments against every CRM record. Build a complete, searchable communication history automatically.

✓

Outlook Integration

Track emails against CRM records directly from Outlook. Server-side sync keeps your activity history current without manual effort.

✓

SharePoint Document Management

Store and access documents directly against accounts, contacts, opportunities, and cases — held in SharePoint, surfaced inside CRM.

✓

Business Process Flows

Step-by-step guided processes for leads, opportunities, and cases ensure your team follows consistent, repeatable workflows every time.

✓

Role-Based Security

Dove365 CRM User and Dove365 CRM Admin roles control exactly what each person can see and do. Built-in governance from day one, no extra configuration required.

✓

Dashboards & Reporting

Built-in management dashboards give you real-time visibility of pipeline, active cases, and team activity — no separate reporting tool needed.
