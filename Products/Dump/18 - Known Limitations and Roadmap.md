# Known Limitations and Roadmap

## Known Limitations

- Dashboard/generative page may need further work or removal if dependent configuration files cause deployment issues.
- AI features are not currently included in base CRM Starter.
- Service Offering fields support CRM categorisation and opportunity-stage value capture, not full sales/invoicing.
- Advanced SLA, entitlement, and escalation automation is not included in the base support module.
- Some platform features require tenant/environment configuration outside solution import, especially SharePoint documents and Outlook tracking.

## Roadmap Ideas

AI-ready roadmap may include:

- Business Skills.
- AI Builder prompts.
- Copilot Studio agents.
- Custom MCP server.
- Lead summaries.
- Opportunity next-step recommendations.
- Case summaries.
- Follow-up email drafting.
- CRM Q&A assistant.

Future sales module may add:

- More advanced service offering and product/service catalogue functionality.
- Quotes, proposals, pricing rules, or invoicing integrations.

Future support module may add:

- SLA and entitlement support.
- Escalation automation.
- Support dashboards and workload reporting.

Future documentation may include:

- Embedded HTML training page inside the model-driven app.
- Screenshot-complete user guides.
- Admin checklist templates.
