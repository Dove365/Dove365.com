Dove365 Support

A structured support and case management product built on Microsoft Power Platform and Dataverse - helping teams manage customer requests, queues, service activity, and resolution performance in one governed system.

Buy Now Request a Demo
What It Is

Bring support work into one clear system

Dove365 Support gives service teams a practical Microsoft-based way to track customers, cases, queues, activities, and service outcomes. It replaces scattered inboxes and spreadsheets with a structured Dataverse app your team can use every day.

01

What it is

A ready-to-configure case management product for Microsoft Power Platform. Cases, customers, contacts, activities, queues, dashboards, and service categories are organised in a single governed model.

02

Who it's for

Designed for small and growing teams that need a more reliable way to manage customer support, internal service requests, managed services work, or operational case handling.

03

Key benefits

Clear ownership, searchable case history, consistent statuses, queue-based working, role-based access, Outlook activity capture, and dashboards that show what needs attention.

04

Common use cases

Customer support desks, managed service teams, account support, internal operations, service request tracking, and teams moving from shared mailboxes into structured case management.

Features

Built for repeatable support delivery

Dove365 Support focuses on the everyday mechanics of good service: capture the request, assign the work, keep context visible, and resolve it with a clear record.

✓

Customer & Contact Records

Keep every organisation and contact connected to their case history, activities, documents, and service context.

✓

Case Management

Track each request from logging through assignment, investigation, resolution, and closure with consistent statuses and ownership.

✓

Queues & Work Allocation

Organise incoming and active work by team, priority, owner, or service area so cases do not disappear inside a shared inbox.

✓

Service Categories

Classify requests by service, type, priority, and source so reporting is consistent and patterns become visible.

✓

Activities & Timeline

Log emails, calls, tasks, notes, and appointments against every case and customer record for a full support history.

✓

Outlook Integration

Track relevant emails from Outlook so conversations stay linked to the right customer and case record.

✓

SharePoint Documents

Store supporting files in SharePoint while surfacing them directly from the customer or case record.

✓

Dashboards & Views

See active cases, overdue work, queue load, priorities, assignments, and service performance without building reports from scratch.

✓

Role-Based Security

Control who can view, manage, administer, and report on support records using Microsoft Dataverse security roles.

✓

Automation-Ready Foundation

Use Power Automate and Copilot Studio to extend triage, notifications, escalations, and knowledge workflows over time.

How Setup Works

From process review to live support desk

Dove365 configures the product around how your team handles support work, then helps you launch with the right roles, views, and operating rhythm.

1

Discovery

Confirm support processes, service categories, queues, team roles, reporting needs, and Microsoft environment readiness.

2

Configuration

Dove365 Support is configured with your case fields, views, queues, categories, roles, and dashboard priorities.

3

Validation

Review real support scenarios and confirm that case capture, assignment, updates, documents, and reporting behave as expected.

4

Launch

Users are provisioned, access is checked, and the support team starts working from the configured product.

5

Handover

Your team receives a practical walkthrough, documentation, and options for ongoing support or continuous improvement.
